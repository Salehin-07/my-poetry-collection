Unknown Crimson

A love poem to a muse never met

By Md Abu Salehin

It was the night of the crimson moon,

I fell in love with her.

The gradient glow she makes

Is far greater than the mighty sun's.

Her rage could burn the fire,

Yet nothing looked more beautiful than her.

Her smile was so sweet

Even the sugar itself would feel shy around her.

❧

She was the one I never met,

Nor do I know her name,

Yet she followed me in my mind

And became the crimson unknown.

She became my crimson moon.
